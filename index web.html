About Tagore Public School

Tamil Nadu's Premier CBSE Educational Institution

Excellence in Education Since 1996

Tagore Public School, established in 1996, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Tamil Nadu. Our institution is recognized as one of the best CBSE schools in Tamil Nadu, offering comprehensive education from Kindergarten to Grade 12.

Tagore Public School in Tamil Nadu implements the comprehensive CBSE curriculum with emphasis on practical learning and competitive exam preparation. Our experienced faculty follows NCERT-based teaching methodologies enhanced with digital learning tools that align with national education policies while maintaining relevance to Tamil Nadu's educational framework [1].

Academic Excellence at Tagore Public School

Our institution follows the comprehensive CBSE curriculum with emphasis on practical learning and application-based education. We prepare students for national-level competitive examinations including engineering and medical entrance tests through specialized coaching programs.

The faculty at Tagore Public School comprises experienced educators who implement NCERT-based teaching methodologies enhanced with digital learning tools. Our academic program aligns with national education policies while maintaining relevance to Tamil Nadu's educational framework.
